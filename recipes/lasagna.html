Lasagna

Learn how to cook delicious lasagna. This recipe includes fresh ingredients and must be followed closely.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Prooducts
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
